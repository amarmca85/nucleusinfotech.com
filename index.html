In 2003 Nucleus Infotech Pvt. Ltd. was a startup – a few smart people with practical experience, no venture capital behind them, working long hours to make good ideas work. Nucleus growth and acceptance can be explained in part by its tight business focus. Today, Nucleus is a premier Data Processing, Consulting and Software Development Company, helping organizations to improve the efficiency & effectiveness of essential business activities through the successful application of IT. Nucleus specializes in delivering a unique blend of technology expertise and business consulting services across multiple industries in India. Our consulting and systems integration expertise spans critical business functions. The best testimonial to the quality of our services is that majority of our revenue comes from repeat business. Information technology has never been more critical to the success of business. Perhaps the most prevalent challenge faced by companies is the lack of qualified resources to meet the increasing demands required of IT departments. To get IT to work for you, Nucleus focuses on assembling a superior technological workforce committed to assist you in obtaining the greatest return on your IT investments and to make sure that your IT department will successfully support your business objectives. Nucleus Infotech Pvt. Ltd. is located in New Delhi, India.
